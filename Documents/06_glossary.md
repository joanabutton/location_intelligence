## BGRI

Base Geográfica de Referenciação da Informação.

The smallest census geography used by the Portuguese National Statistics Institute (INE). A BGRI typically corresponds to a small urban block or cluster of buildings and serves as the unit of analysis throughout this project.

All demographic and opportunity indicators are ultimately attached to BGRI polygons before aggregation and scoring.
